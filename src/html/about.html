Our story

V-TALS is a wholly owned indigenous company specializing in high-quality building materials and home accessories.

Over the years, we have grown into a comprehensive building materials center and a trusted construction supplies provider dedicated to meeting the diverse needs of our clients.

Our core focus is placing the customer first in all our business dealings, ensuring exceptional service and reliable product offerings. With a clear vision and mission to be a center of excellence in the building supplies industry, we aim to consistently surpass customer expectations and deliver generally acceptable solutions that cater to both individual homeowners and large-scale contractors.

As a manufacturer’s representative, we pride ourselves on offering a broad product portfolio that includes genuine building materials such as tiles (Spanish, Italian, Chinese), granites and marbles, security doors, wooden doors, bathroom fixtures like Jacuzzis, bathtubs, shower cubicles, WC units, PVC ceilings, suspended ceilings, kitchen cabinet, stainless steel railings, CCTV security gadgets, glass blocks, water heaters, taps and more.

Our commitment is to provide affordable and durable building supplies and home accessories that are suitable for all classes of individuals. Whether you are undertaking a small renovation or a large construction project, V-TALS is your trusted partner for high-quality building materials and home improvement products.
